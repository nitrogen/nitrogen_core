* Disable Field - #enable {}

	Disable a form field or button. Sets the HTML 'disabled' attribute.

** Usage

#+BEGIN_SRC erlang
	wf:wire(myButton, #disable{}).

	%% or

	wf:disable(myButton).
#+END_SRC
